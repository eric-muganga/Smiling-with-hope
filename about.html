Bringing hope to the less fortunate children.
We are dedicated to Transforming and helping lives of Uganda's orphaned, abandoned, needy, poor and vulnerable children for success.
  • Raising a self-reliable generation that will change Uganda and Africa.
  • An allover improvement in the standards of living and use af modern and practical education.
  • Offering education opportunities to the vulnerable children in the communities of Uganda.
  • Training the young generation on poverty eradication.
  • Building a hospital for the destitute children, community and promoting health care in the community.
  • Establishing and building schools on Christian foundation.
  • To establish a sponsorship base to give education and physical needs to the orphaned, needy and vulnerable children in Uganda.
  • To promote our children talents for a better future.

Founder's Story

Am Kabazzi Daniel Sebuchu born in the rural area of Uganda (Mayuge District), My parents separated when I was 2 years old. It was not until when I was 5 years that my father came back and took me and I grew up with my step mother Am the eldest of all the 8 siblings (6 my Father and 2 my mother)… Although my childhood was full of misery, God is good I received education, completed my university and graduated with a degree in computer science and Information technology from a local university of Uganda. After my mother looking for me and nowhere to be seen with my father for all the 16 years, my father took me to spend my high school holiday of a month with my mother…. Sleeping in a grass thatched mud made hut, Mum could not afford even the most basic needs of life, every day was very hard for me waking up to see very many needy families, kids subjected to child labour, abandoned kids, orphaned kids and helpless poor families like that of my mother could make me cry every day. One night I woke up in a scaring dream like I was in a very big crowd of kids feeding them, talking to them, Encouraging them,nurturing them and God told me ‘’I want you to be the Agent of change to those kids’’ Little did I know that God was calling me to be Smiling With Hope to the Orphaned, abandoned, poor, needy and helpless community.
