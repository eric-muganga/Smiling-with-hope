What is sponsorship?

When you sponsor a child, you care for and encourage them through prayer, letter writing and financial support. That support empowers a local church to individually and holistically meet their needs in a loving, safe community where they can learn, grow, play and dream.

When you become a sponsor, you are personally connected with a child who will know your name and treasure the thought that you care about them.

Your support gives a child life-changing opportunity to develop spiritually, emotionally, physically and economically. It's a relationship more powerful than poverty â€” changing their life and yours!

Your donation and sponsorship provides

Healthy Food

Many children in villages all over Uganda are malnourished because less or no access to food

Pure Water

Over 21 million people in Uganda are living without basic access to safe drinking water.

Health Care

Health systems in Uganda have been criticized for being inequitable, with the poor receiving less services than needed with your help we can provide healthy services to the poor.

Education

Every child has a right to learn and access quality services to fulfil their potential. Education enables gainful employment opportunities and empowers boys and girls to participate in decision-making to build a better future for themselves and their communities.

Residence Facilities

Many children in rural areas all over Uganda are abandoned by their parents at birth hence left homeless and struggling for shelter

Social Care

You can partner with us through encouraging our children by letter writing, visiting us and giving us a hand by parenting at least one child
